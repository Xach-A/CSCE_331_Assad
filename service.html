My Service

As an Aggie, I try to actively give back to my community through food drives, volunteering, and mentoring. Through local organizations and my faith-based community, we hand-make and deliver hundreds of food bags for the less fortunate in Houston.

As an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant on campus, I have the privilege of leading my lab session and helping out fellow students by explaining programming concepts and sharing my experience with them.

I believe that one of the greatest rewards comes through helping others in need and making a positive impact in their lives.
